Py-cloudapp

A drag drop uploader to cloudapp (PyQt)

View project onGitHub

A PyQt based drag drop uploader to Cloud App (http://getcloudapp.com). Upload, Delete and View files right from your system tray. Uploaded files' links get copied to the clipboard automatically.
